Background

problem

ComYoot is trying to reach the student market. ComYoot hopes to use social media to tap into new markets and promote the app to students. They had no coherent visual identity on its social media accounts.

Insight

People who encounter comyoot’s social media and its followers look for different pieces of information. It is important to have a variety of posts to cater to the style of the social media platform.

Outcome

I created the visual identity of ComYoot and lead a team of three to create coherent feeds for Comyoot across Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn.

Solution

Constant communication with client

I created templates of social media posts to ensure consistent visual identity. This was a template that was approved by the CEO of ComYoot. I then drafted social media posts, including graphics, caption, and miscellaneous, to be posted 3x a week. I then responded to feedback from the client and published these posts using HootSuite.

Reflection

Closing thoughts

Having worked on ComYoot for so long, I've grown to know more about the company and its vision. Being involved in ComYoot for so long, I have been challenged to constantly improve my work and look for areas to improve on. ComYoot has enabled me to be business-minded in my designs and think about the end-user and the goal of the company.
